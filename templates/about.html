About Us

Company

Zillionears was formed in the Spring of 2010 by a few college students in Santa Barbara. The concept in the beginning was simple and straightforward: Assist great artists in acquiring the listeners they deserve, as well as guiding music loving fans towards great new artists. This built a strong foundation with nothing but possibilities and potential to grow and innovate on. Our first year was spent getting to know the needs and wants of both the musicians and fans to better understand and envision the final product. To do this we created a platform online to feature and share unsigned artists and their work. Our second year was spent developing and fine-tuning what is now known as the “Social Sale.” Our mission is to give artists a direct-to-fan platform to release content in a new creative way. We strive to create a tool for artists to engage their current fans and drive interest from new fans.
